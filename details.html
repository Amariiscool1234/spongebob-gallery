SpongeBob SquarePants

SpongeBob SquarePants is a cheerful and energetic sea sponge who lives in Bikini Bottom. He works as a fry cook at the Krusty Krab and is known for his optimism, hard work, and love for jellyfishing. He spends most of his time with his best friend Patrick Star and his neighbor Squidward.

Details

  • Universe : SpongeBob Squarepants
  • Role: A Fry Cook
  • Species: Sea Sponge

Character's Backstory

Spongebob lives in Bikini Bottom. His best friend is Patrick. Spongebob also works at the Krusty Krab as a fry cook.

He spends most of the time doing his job, hanging out with his friend patrick, and spending time with his pet snail Gary.

Personality

Spongebob is known as being very energetic, friendly, and optimistic. He really cares about his friends.

Abilities

Spongebob is very skilled at making Krabby Patties. He is also very skilled at jellyfishing.
