This is Spongebob & Friends

Spongebob Squarepants is a cheerful, energetic yellow sea sponge who resides in Bikini Bottom. He works at the restaurant Krusty Krab as a skilled fry cook. He loves to go jellyfishing and enjoys spending time with his best friend, Patrick Star.
